RFID Lock

This week, I built off of my project last week and incorporated a solenoid into the RFID system to make an RFID lock. Despite the OLED already being an output device, I wanted another way to display whether someone had access or not. Because of time restrictions, I did not fully build a lock box, I simply hooked up the solenoid to the already existing system and controlled it via the RFID cards and reader from last week.

Materials Needed:

  • ESP32-Mini
  • RFID-RC522
  • Breadboard
  • Pin to Pin wires
  • OLED display
  • Arduino IDE
  • Mini Solenoid



    • The subtle clicking that you hear in the video is the solenoid engaging and disengaging.
    I think that I would love to get to develop this project a little further, maybe by making a little latch that the solenoid could hook into.
